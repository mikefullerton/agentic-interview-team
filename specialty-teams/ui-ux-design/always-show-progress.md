## Worker Focus
Determinate progress (bar with percentage) when total work is known; indeterminate (spinner, skeleton, shimmer) when not; no frozen or unresponsive UI

## Verify
Every async operation shows progress; no blank/frozen screens; progress type matches whether work total is known
