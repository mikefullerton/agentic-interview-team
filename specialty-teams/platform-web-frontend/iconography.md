## Worker Focus
Use SVG icons with accessible names (`aria-label` or `<title>`), consistent size/weight classes, minimum 24x24px interactive, status icons paired with color + shape or text

## Verify
All icon buttons have `aria-label`; decorative icons have `aria-hidden="true"`; interactive icons ≥24x24px; status icons not color-only
