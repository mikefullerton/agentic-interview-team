## Worker Focus
Build only for today's known requirements; no speculative abstractions, hooks for future features, or generalization beyond current need; cost of premature abstraction (ongoing maintenance) exceeds cost of adding it later when needed

## Verify
No unused abstraction layers, extension points, or generalization not required by current features; code complexity matches current requirements
