## Structured Questions

1. What frontend framework are you using — React, Vue, Svelte, vanilla? Why that choice?

2. How do you handle responsive design? Mobile-first? Breakpoints? What's your smallest supported viewport?

3. What's your CSS strategy — utility classes (Tailwind), CSS modules, styled components, or something else?

4. How do you manage client-side state? Redux, Zustand, Context, URL state? Where does each type of state live?

5. What's your approach to server-side rendering vs. client-side rendering? Hydration strategy?

6. How do you handle browser compatibility? What's your browser support matrix? Polyfills?

7. Describe your bundle size strategy. Code splitting? Lazy loading? Tree shaking? What's your target bundle size?

8. How do you handle forms — controlled components, form libraries, native form validation?

9. What's your image strategy — srcset, lazy loading, CDN, WebP/AVIF? How do you handle responsive images?

10. How do you manage routing? Client-side, server-side, or hybrid? How do URLs map to content?

11. What's your SEO strategy if applicable? Meta tags, structured data, sitemap?

12. How do you test your frontend — unit tests (Jest/Vitest), component tests (Testing Library), E2E (Playwright/Cypress)?

## Exploratory Prompts

1. If JavaScript was disabled, what would your app show? Is progressive enhancement important for your use case?

2. What if your target audience is primarily on mobile browsers with slow connections? How would that change your architecture?

3. How do you handle the tension between rich interactivity and page load performance?

4. If you had to support a screen reader perfectly, which component in your app would be hardest to make accessible?
